# Employee Onboarding Guide

## First Week Checklist

New employees should complete payroll setup, benefits enrollment, security awareness training, and manager introductions during their first week. Managers are expected to schedule a role overview session within the first three business days.

## Equipment and Access

IT issues laptops on the employee's start date and provides access to email, chat, the HR portal, and project management tools. Requests for specialized software should be submitted through the internal service desk and approved by the hiring manager.

## Training Expectations

Each new hire is assigned a 30-day ramp plan. The plan includes mandatory compliance modules, product orientation, and shadowing sessions with a peer mentor. Completion status is reviewed with the manager at the end of the first month.

## Probationary Period

All new full-time employees have a 90-day probationary period. At the end of that period, the manager submits a probation review summarizing performance, role fit, and any recommended support actions.
