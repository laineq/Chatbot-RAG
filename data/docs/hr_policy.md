# HR Policy Handbook

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off per calendar year during their first two years. Beginning in year three, the annual balance increases to 20 days. Time off requests should be submitted through the HR portal at least five business days before the planned absence, except in emergencies.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave each calendar year. Sick leave can be used for personal illness, preventive care, or to care for an immediate family member. Managers may request documentation for absences longer than three consecutive workdays.

## Performance Reviews

Formal performance reviews are conducted twice per year, in June and December. Employees are expected to complete a self-assessment one week before the review cycle begins, and managers must submit a written evaluation before the review meeting.

## Remote Work Expectations

Employees approved for hybrid or remote work must remain available during their assigned core collaboration window from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in their local time zone. Team leads may require in-person attendance for quarterly planning meetings with at least two weeks of notice.
