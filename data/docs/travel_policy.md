# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## Booking Expectations

Employees should use the approved travel booking portal whenever possible. Flights should be booked at least 14 days in advance unless travel is approved as urgent by a department director. Economy class is the default for domestic flights under six hours.

## Hotel Expenses

Hotel reimbursement is capped at 250 USD per night before taxes in standard markets. For high-cost cities such as New York, San Francisco, and London, the cap increases to 350 USD per night before taxes with manager approval noted in the expense report.

## Meals and Incidentals

Business travel meals are reimbursable up to 75 USD per day in total. Alcohol is not reimbursable unless it is part of an approved client entertainment event and specifically noted in the submitted expense report.

## Ground Transportation

Ride-share, taxi, rail, and airport shuttle expenses are reimbursable when they are the most practical option. Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at the current company mileage rate of 0.62 USD per mile when mileage is logged with origin, destination, and business purpose.

## Receipt Requirements

Receipts are required for all travel expenses above 25 USD. Expense reports must be submitted within 10 calendar days after the trip ends. Missing receipts over the threshold require a manager-approved exception note.
